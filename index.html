Discover Divine Trails

Kedarnath – Lord Shiva's Abode

Kedarnath is one of the most revered pilgrimage destinations of Lord Shiva nestled in the Himalayas. The journey involves a scenic and spiritual trek, attracting lakhs of devotees every year.

Badrinath – Sacred Land of Vishnu

Badrinath, located on the banks of Alaknanda River, is dedicated to Lord Vishnu. It's one of the Char Dhams and a must-visit destination for spiritual seekers and nature lovers alike.

Tungnath – Highest Shiva Temple

Tungnath is the highest Shiva temple in the world and part of the Panch Kedar pilgrimage. The trail to Tungnath offers stunning views of the Himalayas and leads further to Chandrashila peak.
